Our Values

Our work is guided by the key values:

Our relation with Humanity We Serve

  1. he patient always comes first. We are dedicated to patient care.
  2. We treat each person with respect and dignity.
  3. We are compassionate listeners. We hear the issues of our patients, respect them, and do everything in our power to help.
  4. We provide patient-centered service. People who come to the Siddiqui hijama & Herbal can expect polite, friendly helpful staff members who relate to each person as an individual, recognizing their history, relationships, culture, and needs. We go the extra mile to meet their expectations.
  5. We believe that patients deserve to have timely access to health care and that our systems should reflect this value.
  6. We seek direction from patients through their guidance as members of our Board; we seek direction from other contacts using multiple methods.

Our Approach to Health Care

  1. We care for the whole person, see the complexity of each person’s life, and believe that addressing a broad range of human needs is the best way to improve a person’s health.
  2. We believe that providing high quality, accessible health care is our reason for being. We seek to be a model for other community health centers.
  3. We use a team approach to providing health care, and involve the patient as part of our team.

Our Relationship with the Community

  1. We have a commitment to serving the community and providing open access to Siddiqui Hijama & Herbal for all community members.
  2. Involvement by our staff in the community enhances our ability to provide effective health care. Improving the community will improve the health of our customers.
  3. By instilling confidence in our patients, they will become positive forces in the community and contribute to the health of others.

Our Work Environment

  1. In all we do, we actively pursue excellence and search for the next level of accomplishment.
  2. We take pride in our work.
  3. We create a dynamic, forward-moving, innovative organization.
  4. Our integrity and ethics will never be compromised.
  5. We have a strong work ethic, yet we don’t stifle our individual personalities.
  6. We share a common mission of serving others through spirit-based leadership.
  7. Teamwork is central to our work. We each take the responsibility to contribute effectively to teams.
